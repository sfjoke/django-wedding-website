Kocanda Kravsko

Getting There

Kravsko is a village located near a beautiful wine city called Znojmo. It is reachable from Znojmo by bus (bus nr. 805, stop called Kravsko, rozcestí k závodu and then walk approximately 12 minutes to Kocanda Kravsko) or by taxi (https://taxi-znojmo.cz/) and easy to access by car as well.
