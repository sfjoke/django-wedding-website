Kocanda Kravsko

Getting There

Kravsko is a village located near a beautiful wine city called Znojmo in Czech Republic.

The wedding venue is in Kocanda Kravsko. For more information about the place, see here: https://www.kocandakravsko.cz/

It is reachable from Znojmo by bus (bus nr. 805, stop called Kravsko, Rozcestí k Závodu and then walk approximately 12 minutes to Kocanda Kravsko) or by taxi (https://taxi-znojmo.cz/) and easy to access by car as well.
